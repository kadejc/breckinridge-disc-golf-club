Course Info

Breckinridge Park Disc Golf Course was designed by 2010 World Champion Eric McCabe and opened on November 21, 2023 — Richardson's first disc golf course. It sits on Breckinridge Park's 417-acre grounds, adjacent to Parking Lot C off Brand Road, and is open sunrise to sunset, seven days a week. The course came about as a result of community input received during the 2022 update of Richardson's Parks, Recreation and Open Space Master Plan.

18 holes, par 55, total length 6,377 ft.
