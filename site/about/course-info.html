Course Info

Tucked into the 417 acres of Breckinridge Park, our course was designed by 2010 World Champion Eric McCabe and opened November 21, 2023 as Richardson's first disc golf course — built in response to community input gathered during the city's 2022 Parks, Recreation and Open Space Master Plan update. You'll find it just off Brand Road, next to Parking Lot C, open sunrise to sunset every day of the week.
